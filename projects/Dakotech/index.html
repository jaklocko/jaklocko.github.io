About Us

Our Beginnings

Dakotech was founded in 1999 in Detroit, MI as a small part-time Information Technology consulting and support business. In 2003, Dakotech moved to Ann Arbor, MI to better serve the Southeastern Michigan area as we felt that it would be best to position ourselves in a technology corridor. What started out as two employees driving around door-to-door in an old Volkswagon soon became a full-time operation catering primarily to law firms, professional offices, and other small businesses.

Having had the fortune to land some of our most dedicated and successful clients in the first few years of business, we have carried some of the top law offices to the very edge of high technology. We strive to maintain the highest level of customer service, ethical billing, and a leading-edge knowledge base. We understand that not everyone is as attuned to technology as we are, and we do what it takes to get you excited about the terrifying prospects of technology.
